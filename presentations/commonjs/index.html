Reveal.js Remote

Sharing a presentation

  • Press a to get a share URL for the audience
  • Open that link as often as you want
  • When the presenter changes the slides, the followers are updated

Sharing a presentation

  • Sharing requires that the follower have access to the presentation
  • The Node.js server that syncs the presentations can also host them!

Controlling a presentation

  • Press R (share) to get a control URL
  • Do not share that url with the audience!
  • Open the URL on a smartphone
  • You can see the speaker notes and presentation controls on that second screen
Speaker notes are transferred to the second screen!
